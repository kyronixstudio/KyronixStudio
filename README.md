### Who we are

KyronixStudio is a small independent studio. We take projects from idea to shipped product — websites, bots, SaaS tools, automation. We don't do half-finished work.

---

### What we build

**Websites & Web Apps**
Full-stack sites and web apps. Fast, clean, and actually maintained after launch.

**Discord Bots**
Custom bots built for real servers — moderation, economy, tickets, leveling, whatever you need.

**SaaS Products**
Full platforms with auth, dashboards, billing, and APIs. Not just a demo — a real product.

**Automation & Tools**
Internal tools, scrapers, and backend systems that cut out repetitive work.
